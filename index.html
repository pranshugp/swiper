Nature

Mountain

They usually have steep, sloping sides and sharp or rounded ridges, and a high point, called a peak or summit.

Waterfall

A waterfall is a river or other body of water's steep fall over a rocky ledge into a plunge pool below. Waterfalls are also called cascades.

Forest

Forests cover one third of the earth's land mass, performing vital functions and supporting the livelihoods of 1.6 billion people.

Beach

A beach is a narrow strip of land separating a body of water from inland areas.
